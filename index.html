Frequently Asked Questions

How long does trademark registration take in India?

Typically, the process takes 18-24 months if there are no objections. Trumarx expedites filing and promptly handles examination reports to minimize delays.

Do you handle international patent filing?

Yes. We specialize in PCT (Patent Cooperation Treaty) applications, allowing you to seek patent protection in over 150 countries simultaneously.

What industries do you serve?

We serve a diverse range of industries including Information Technology, Entertainment & Media, Manufacturing, Life Sciences, and Retail.

Can I trademark a name before I start my business?

Yes! You can file on a "Proposed to be Used" basis. This secures your priority date and protects your brand name before you even launch.

What is the difference between ™ and ®?

The ™ symbol is used for unregistered trademarks to claim rights. The ® symbol is reserved ONLY for officially registered trademarks. Using ® without a registration certificate is illegal.

What if someone copies my trademark?

We help you enforce your rights through cease-and-desist notices and litigation. Trademark registration gives you the legal power to stop infringement and claim damages.

How is Trumarx different from other firms?

We don't just file paperwork; we provide strategic IP counsel. With over 20 years of experience and a 98% success rate, we actively monitor and protect your brand asset, acting as your long-term legal partner.
